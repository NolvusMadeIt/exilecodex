## Early Access Launch

- Path of Exile 2 entered Early Access as version 0.1.0 — the game's public debut.
- Launched with the opening Acts of the campaign, the first set of character classes and their Ascendancies, and the foundations of the endgame Atlas.
- Established the core systems the game is built on: the reworked skill-gem and support system, dodge-roll combat, the passive skill tree, and the crafting currency that later patches expanded.

_0.1.0 was the Early Access launch build. Every patch after it is listed here with its full notes._
