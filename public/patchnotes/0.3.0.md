## Act Four

- The Third Edict adds Act 4, continuing the storyline from Act 3.
- Journey to Kingsmarch, hire a ship, and set sail to find an ancient weapon.
- Discover the Ngamakanui archipelago with eight islands to explore.
- Act Four is completely non-linear; visit islands in any order.

## Interludes

- Removal of Cruel Difficulty.
- Three new temporary interludes take players to level 65.
- Endgame begins at level 65.

## Rise of the Abyssals League

- New economy with a fresh-start league. Free passive tree refund granted to Early Access characters.
- Abyssals emerge through fissures across Wraeclast. Slaying monsters triggers Abyssal emergence with necromantic abilities.
- Close Abysses to reveal Abyssal troves and underground access.
- Find ancient bones to imbue items with abyssal magic; use the Well of Souls to reveal and transform Desecrated modifiers.
- Delve into Abyssal Depths for undead legions and powerful rewards; challenge Abyssal commanders at the Ancient Spire.
- Standard, Hardcore, and Solo Self-Found variations available.

## Major Character Balance Overhaul

- Significant changes across entire game character balance, aimed at increasing build diversity with more viable skills and archetypes.

## Support Gem System Overhaul

- Removed the restriction of one support gem of each type per character.
- Many supports now have multiple tiers with different values and effects.
- Added 11 new Support Gems and introduced 40 Lineage Supports for endgame only.
- Renamed several supports for clearer functionality; Lightning/Cold/Fire/Chaos Infusion renamed to Attunement.
- Supports for "skills you use yourself" cannot support Persistent Skills.

## New Content and Features

- Added new Strength, Dexterity and Intelligence skills (Fortifying Cry, Forge Hammer, Ancestral Cry, Toxic Domain, Ice-Tipped Arrows, Frost Darts and more).
- Added persistent buffs including Iron Ward, Mortar Cannon, Mirage Archer, Ravenous Swarm and Siphon Elements; Grim Feast reworked.
- Added new keystones: Hollow Palm Technique, Ritual Cadence, Blackflame Covenant, Walker of the Wilds.
- Added Greater and Perfect versions of Transmutation, Augmentation, Chaos, Regal and Exalted Orbs.
- Added Hinekora's Lock currency (foresee the next currency result).
- Added over 10 new Unique items, over 50 new base item types, over 20 new Endgame Maps, and 15 new Omens.

## Endgame Changes

- Pinnacle content now drops Splinters instead of requiring combined keys; insert Splinters into the Realmgate to select difficulty.
- Atlas Passive Trees no longer affect content difficulty, only map content.
- Arbiter of Ash is now static difficulty with infinite portals; added Uber Arbiter of Ash requiring Calamity Fragments from Citadels.
- Waystone modifiers reworked with downsides, upsides and drop chance; Corrupted Waystones access the Corrupted Nexus.
- High level items can have up to 30% quality; rare extra sockets possible in high tier maps.

## Essence Rework

- Essences now have 4 tiers: Lesser, Normal, Greater, Perfect, adding specific modifiers based on tier and item type.
- Lesser/Normal/Greater can upgrade Magic items to Rare with a guaranteed modifier; Perfect and Corrupted Essences modify Rare items.
- Added 7 new Essence types.

## Player Changes

- Hold the dodge-roll button to sprint after the roll completes; sprint is available on all classes with no time limit.
- Cannot socket multiple of the same Skill Gem in main sockets.
- Player Evasion works on all hits except red-flash boss skills; evasion formula adjusted.
- Attribute requirements lowered ~20–25% on equipment and Skill Gems at endgame.
- Fire Damage no longer has inherent ignite chance; it inflicts a Flammability debuff instead (multiple hits stack independently).
- Shock now lasts 8 seconds and Chill 8 seconds on non-players; chills below 30% magnitude are discarded.
- Players gain +6 Accuracy per level; Dexterity grants +6 Accuracy per point.
- Weapon swapping is now instant; player projectile skills always fire at least one projectile.

## Ascendancy Changes

- Wide reworks across many Ascendancies (Warbringer, Smith of Kitava, Gemling Legionnaire, Tactician, Witchhunter, Ritualist, Amazon, Pathfinder, Deadeye, Invoker, Acolyte of Chayula, Blood Mage, Infernalist, Lich, Chronomancer, Stormweaver) — see the in-game tree for each node's exact wording.

## Passive Skill Tree Changes

- Elemental Equilibrium redesigned; Resolute Technique now doubles Accuracy; Acrobatics keystone removed.
- Added 124 new clusters across the tree; Area of Effect sources lowered ~25%.
- Many existing clusters updated with forced refunds for affected allocations.

## Skill, Unique and Item Changes

- Extensive rebalancing of base damage, mana costs, and minion scaling across the skill roster.
- Numerous Unique items adjusted; Distilled Emotions renamed to Liquid Emotions (tiered Diluted/Concentrated).
- Martial Weapon modifiers and implicits adjusted; Orb of Chance more likely to succeed on Amulets, Rings, Belts and Charms.
