## The Dawn of the Hunt League

- Start fresh in a new economy with the Dawn of the Hunt league. Old Early Access characters remain available with free passive tree refunds due to changes.
- Standard, Hardcore and Solo Self-Found variations available.

## New Content and Features

- Added new playable character class: the Huntress, with Amazon and Ritualist Ascendancies.
- Added Smith of Kitava (Warrior), Tactician (Mercenary) and Lich (Witch) Ascendancy Classes.
- Added over 25 new Skills (primarily Spear Skills) and over 100 new Supports.
- Added 2 new item classes: Spears and Bucklers.
- Added over 100 new Unique Items and 8 new Endgame Maps with new encounters and rewards.
- Introduced 4 Unique Strongboxes, 12 Rogue Exiles (each dropping unique items), and Azmerian Wisps that empower monsters.
- Introduced the Twilight Reliquary Key for accessing The Reliquary Vault with special foil Unique items.
- Essences can now have Vaal Orbs applied for corruption with unpredictable outcomes.

## Endgame Changes

- Corruption areas on the Atlas now have a central Corrupted Nexus as the affliction source; cleansing it grants Crystalline Shards for Atlas Passive points.
- Added the Fracturing Orb (fractures and locks a modifier on a rare item with at least 4 modifiers).
- Players can now respawn and return to Maps after dying (up to 6 attempts per Map initially); respawns decrease as more Waystone modifiers are added.
- Tower count drastically reduced but each is now more powerful; Tablets add more mechanics at higher tiers.
- Waystone acquisition redesigned for more reliable sustain; final map bosses drop a Waystone one tier higher.
- New Recombination crafting mechanic in Expedition lets you select mods from two items.
- Pinnacle bosses are much easier at Difficulty 0; Difficulty 4 preserved. Pinnacle unlock time roughly halved.
- Players no longer lose or gain experience in certain arena/encounter areas (Simulacrum, Twisted Domain, etc.).

## Atlas Passive Tree Changes

- Added support for Azmerian Spirits and Rogue Exiles; more Notables added and reworked.
- You can now obtain 40 Atlas Passive Skill points (previously 30).

## Player Changes

- Characters require level 25 for Cruel difficulty and level 50 for Endgame.
- Unified stun mechanics between players and monsters; "stagger" renamed to heavy stun (3-second duration on players).
- Player Ailment Threshold is now half of Maximum Life; Presence base radius reduced to 4 metres.
- Freeze builds ~48% slower on enemies; charge base duration reduced to 15 seconds.
- Fully Broken Armour causes 20% increased Physical Damage taken.

## Ascendancy, Passive Tree, Skill and Support Changes

- Extensive reworks across Titan, Warbringer, Gemling Legionnaire, Witchhunter, Deadeye, Pathfinder, Acolyte of Chayula, Invoker, Blood Mage, Infernalist, Chronomancer and Stormweaver.
- New passive clusters for Blind, Volatility, Companions, Presence/Aura, Parry, Banners, Bleeding, Thorns and Quivers; new keystones Crimson Assault and Trusted Kinship.
- Base damage, mana costs and minion scaling rebalanced across a huge number of skills and supports.

## Unique and Item Changes

- Many Unique items rebalanced (Astramentis, Atziri's Disdain, Ingenuity, Morior Invictus, Temporalis, Queen of the Forest and more).
- Jeweller's Orbs now set Support Gem sockets instead of adding them.
- Runes consolidated into 3 tiers (Lesser/Regular/Greater) with doubled drop rate.
- Rarity modifier caps reduced; numerous base-item and modifier adjustments.

## Monster, Quest, UI & Microtransaction Changes

- Boss life and damage rebalanced (pinnacle bosses roughly half life at Difficulty 0); all monster Armour reduced 30%.
- Quest flow tweaks (Ascent to Power, Earning Passage); many UI improvements including co-op spectating and extensive controller support.
- Enabled a large batch of cosmetic armour sets, portals, pets and hideout items.

## Bug Fixes

- A very large batch of fixes across skills, supports, minions, modifiers, encounters, the Atlas and the UI.
