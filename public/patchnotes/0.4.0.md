## The Fate of the Vaal League

- Start fresh in a new economy with The Fate of the Vaal league
- All old characters from Early Access remain in Early Access leagues
- Free passive tree refund granted due to changes
- In the Fate of the Vaal league, find remnants of ancient Vaal civilization throughout Wraeclast
- Corruption has returned; long-dead Vaal structures activate and affect nearby creatures
- Sacrifice afflicted monsters to power devices and travel to Lira Vaal, the ancient temple of Atziri
- Gather power to modify the Temple and create desired layout
- Each room offers rewards from currency stashes to unique items
- Placing rooms next to beneficial rooms levels them up, adding new challenges and rewards
- Complete more beacons to fuel additional temple additions
- Explore the Queen of the Vaal's opulent temple during height of her power
- Standard, Hardcore and Solo Self-Found variations available
- Private league versions can be created

## New Content and Features

- Added new playable character class: the Druid
- Added Oracle and Shaman Ascendancy Classes for the Druid
- Added over 20 new Skills, primarily Primal Skills
- Added over 35 new Supports
- Introduced over 8 new Lineage Supports, powerful Supports available only in Endgame
- Added over 250 new passive skills to the passive tree
- Added over 10 new Unique Items
- Added new weapon class: Talismans (two-handed martial weapons for shapeshifting attacks)
  - Equipping a talisman shapeshifts into animal form
  - Grants basic attack skill for that form
  - Swap chosen form at any time
- Added 4 new Augment items with new modifiers for crafting
- Continued incremental improvements to sound, art, effects and environments

## Abyss as a Core Mechanic

- Abyss added to core game
- Find Abyss encounters in specific campaign areas starting in Act 2
- Abyss encounters added to Endgame, Tablets and related modifiers
- Added Atlas Passive Tree for Rise of the Abyssal Content
- Preserved Vertebrae no longer drop
- Updated The Well of Souls quest to account for Abysses no longer spawning in all campaign areas

## General Improvements

- Significant CPU performance optimizations implemented
- Changes improve speed of key operations and utilize more CPU cores
- Results in higher frame rates and significantly fewer frame spikes when CPU bound
- Overall minimum 25% boost expected, especially impactful for heavy gameplay situations
- GPU-bound users will not see improvement; lower render resolution or use Dynamic Resolution instead
