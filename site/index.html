what ships today

Five tools, one overlay.

Everything below ships in the build you download today — one overlay, five tools, always right where you need them.

Market Companion

The Currency Exchange, live on your desk.

Real prices, 24-hour moves, volume and sparklines for every currency — with buy / sell signals and full candlestick history, so you know what a Divine is worth before you trade it.

  • Live prices & 24h % from the Currency Exchange
  • Volume, trend signals and 24h / 7d / 30d charts
  • Watchlist the currencies you actually farm

Campaign Guide

A living leveling route that follows you from zone to zone — every act, every reward, moving itself forward as you play and quietly waiting while you're in town.

Guide Forge

Build your own step-by-step guides — real goals (kill, talk, collect…), counts, level triggers and image links — then run them in the same overlay.

Run Tracker

A LiveSplit-style speedrun timer that splits itself on zones and levels, strips out load time, and tracks your personal bests and gold splits — completely hands-free.

Crafting Bench

An interactive PoE2 crafting simulator: pick a base, apply currency, essences and omens, watch the mod pool and weights, roll and backtrack.

Build Planner

The real Path of Building, running right inside the overlay — the full PoE2 passive tree, skills, items and live calculations. No alt-tab, no second app.
