how it works

It watches the game so you don't have to.

01

Overlay, not alt-tab

ExileCodex pins over Path of Exile 2 as a true game overlay. Read your guide, check a price or glance at your splits without ever leaving the fight.

02

Driven by your log

It reads the same Client.txt the game already writes — your character, level and zone. The guide follows you from area to area; the timer starts and pauses on its own.

03

Safe by design

No memory reads. No input automation. Only your log and your own screen — squarely inside Grinding Gear Games' rules for third-party tools.
