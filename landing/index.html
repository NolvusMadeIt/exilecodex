Why PoE2 Champions

Everything a filter needs — none of the syntax

Built for new players and veterans alike: every control is wired to clean, in-game-valid PoE2 filter output.

Visual first

Pick item types from dropdowns with real PoE2 art. You never write a Class == line by hand.

Live output

A docked panel regenerates valid filter syntax on every change. What you see is what loads in-game.

In-game overlay

Pin the studio over PoE2 in Borderless and toggle it with a hotkey — no alt-tabbing mid-map.

Import filters & builds

Load a standard PoE2 .filter or a build export — it bakes into editable filter settings you can keep tuning.

Quick Filters

Toggle what matters in seconds

Grouped image dropdowns for currency, gear, flasks, gems and more. Flip a switch to show or hide; tune thresholds with sliders.

  • Hide chaff, surface upgrades
  • Class- and progress-aware defaults
  • Expand or collapse everything at once
Cosmetic

Make the loot you want impossible to miss

Per value tier: text colour, background, borders, light beams, minimap icons and drop sounds — audition each sound before you commit.

  • Beams & minimap icons per tier
  • Audible alerts you can preview
  • Three hand-tuned dark themes
New · Desktop app

Your filter, on top of the game

The Windows app pins the studio over Path of Exile 2 in Borderless mode and slides it in or out with a hotkey — default Shift+Alt+F. Edit between packs, hide it, keep playing.

  • Docks left or right, ~38% wide & ~90% tall
  • Multi-monitor aware — lands on the screen you play on
  • Custom frameless window & system-tray icon

A look around

The whole studio

Presets, Quick Filters, Cosmetic styling and a live Preview that renders real in-game item labels — plus optional plugins: a live Price Check, a currency Market, and a Campaign & Speedrun guide with zone maps, live game-log tracking and a run timer.

Add-ons

Plugins that do more

Optional, toggleable add-ons in the desktop app — turn on only what you want. The browser version is a free preview; the live plugins run in the Windows app.

Price Check

Paste an item and get its live sell price straight from the official trade — shown with the real currency orb icons, through your own logged-in session. Desktop.

Market

A live PoE2 currency market — browse every currency, chart its price (24h → all), and price in Exalted or Divine with plain-English buy/sell signals. Desktop.

Campaign Mode

A zone-by-zone leveling guide with a real layout map for nearly every zone, hover-a-zone-name map popups, and live game-log tracking that follows you and ticks the route as you play.

Speedrun Mode

The lean critical-path route plus a pre-run prep checklist and a live run timer that pauses in town, hideout or when you're idle — with act splits, a personal best and rebindable hotkeys.
