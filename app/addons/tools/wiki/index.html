Waystone search regex — a plain guide

The in-game stash and vendor search box can match text against the mods on your items. A "regex" is just a search pattern. On Waystones (and Tablets) it's the fastest way to find the ones worth running and skip the rest — no squinting at every mod line.

The five rules that matter

  • It matches any text on the item — mod lines, names — and it's not case-sensitive.
  • Spaces count. The search treats a space like any other character, so pack siz and packsiz are different. Keep your spacing exact.
  • 250 characters max per search. The builder shows your count so you don't overrun it.
  • | means OR. "rarity|pack siz" matches a Waystone that has Item Rarity or Pack Size.
  • Two quoted groups mean AND, and ! in front of a group excludes it. "rarity" !"extra fire" = has Rarity, but hide the extra-fire ones.

Why it helps

When your stash is full of Waystones, you want the juicy ones fast: high Item Rarity, big Pack Size, more Magic/Rare monsters — and you want to steer clear of dangerous rolls like extra elemental damage or big monster-life boosts. A good regex highlights exactly those in one keystroke.

Worked examples

"rarity|pack siz"Highlight Waystones with Item Rarity or Pack Size.
"rarity" "pack siz"Only Waystones that have both (each quoted group must match).
"magic mons|rare mons"Extra monster density — more packs to kill.
"rarity" !"extra fire"Rarity rolls, but exclude ones that deal extra Fire damage.
"m rar.*([6-9].|\d..)%"Advanced: Waystones with 60%+ Item Rarity (numeric matching).

Using this builder

  1. Toggle the mods you want under Loot & rewards and Monster density — green chips are included and joined with |.
  2. Toggle mods under Exclude to hide dangerous rolls — they get wrapped in !"…" for you.
  3. The box at the top shows the finished regex and its character count. Hit Copy and paste it into the in-game search.
  4. Save preset keeps a setup (e.g. "juice", "safe farm") on this device so you can reload it later.
